About Me

As a dedicated Technical Specialist with 13 years of industrial expertise, I redefine quality through my mastery of technical cleanliness in the automotive sector. Over the past 7 years, I have led cleanliness laboratories, conducted rigorous cleanroom audits, and developed innovative contamination control strategies, ensuring compliance with ISO 14644 and ISO 16232/VDA 19.1 standards.

My expertise in particle characterization, material characterization, metallurgical analysis, and failure analysis enables me to deliver unparalleled material insights. Passionate about training cross-functional teams, I foster collaboration and drive excellence. A proactive innovator, I am committed to maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness.
